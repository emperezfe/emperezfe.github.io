Necrognomicon

Action and tower defense game about an army of possessed garden gnomes taking over a small town.

Overview

  • Role: Main game designer
  • Platform: PC (itch.io)
  • Engine: Unreal Engine 4
  • Team: +25 people
  • Dates: From December 2019 to July 2020

What I did

  • Systems design, AI design, UI/UX design, game feel.
  • Ranged combat design, level design.
  • Arcade mode & endless mode design.
  • User tests; data analysis with Power BI.

Selected examples

Core loop & readability. Enemy spawns, ammo/ability economy, and feedback layers tuned for clarity and pace.

  • Hit-stop, screen shake, VFX/SFX layers.
  • Design of several weapons and projectiles.
  • Horde system design.
  • Character movement design.
  • Telemetry to iterate balance in short loops.

Arcade & endless modes. Scoring, multipliers, waves and pacing knobs to keep challenge and flow.

  • A month before finishing the project, I reused most systems to create an arcade mode.
